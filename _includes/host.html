Hosting a Site

If you'd like to host a site, the requirements are simple:

  • Email the Mentoring Committee and let us know right away, so we can get you set up.
  • Find some space for people to meet that's accessible, and can accommodate lots of people with laptops.
  • Reach out to your community to let them know what's happening.
  • Arrange for a video connection to the global Google Hangout on Air, so your site can lead a session on the broadcast sessions list above.
  • Brainstorm with your community on what kind of broadcast session you'd all like to lead; we'd like every site to try to lead a one-hour session, if possible.
  • Create a schedule for local practice teaching sessions - encourage everyone to try something new!
